EVIDENCE BASED TEACHING


Evidence Based Teaching 1 (EBT) – 2 ½ day course



Enhance you trainer skills with this trainer development course, delegates will gain an understanding of the principles of evidence based teaching. Participants will gain the ability to utilise EBT techniques that evidence has shown to produce the biggest impact in student achievement.

Evidence Based Teaching 2 (EBT) – 2 ½ day course



EBT 2 is designed to compliment and build on the skills learnt from EBT 1. Participants will gain a deeper understanding of the principles of EBT, allowing them to develop their own methods tailored to suit the unique requirements of your organisation. We will explore more in depth classroom theories and understand the importance of differentiation within our student cohort.
